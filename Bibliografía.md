Estructuras de Datos
====================

Bibliografía
------------

El curso sigue cercanamente el siguiente libro:

* Peláez, Canek: *Estructuras de Datos con Java Moderno: Comportamiento +
  Objetos = Programas*. Las Prensas de Ciencias, Facultad de Ciencias, UNAM,
  México, 2018, ISBN 9786073009669.

el cual pueden encontrar en Servicios Editoriales de la Facultad o en [la tienda
virtual](https://tienda.fciencias.unam.mx/) de la misma, tanto en [forma
impresa](https://tienda.fciencias.unam.mx/es/home/437-estructuras-de-datos-con-java-moderno-9786073009157.html)
como
[digital](https://tienda.fciencias.unam.mx/es/home/438-estructuras-de-datos-con-java-moderno-9786073009666.html).

La siguiente bibliografía es de cualquier forma recomendada:

* Adelson-Velsky, Georgy y Evgenii Landis: *An algorithm for the organization of
  information*. En *Proceedings of the USSR Academy of Sciences*, volumen 146,
  páginas 263-266, 1962. English translation by Myron J. Ricci in Soviet
  Math. Doklady, 3:1259-1263.
* Bayer, Rudolf: *Symmetric binary B-trees: Data structure and maintenance
  algorithms*. Acta informatica, 1(4):290-306, 1972.
* Bayer, Rudolf y Edward McCreight: *Organization and Maintenance of Large
  Ordered Indices*. En *Proceedings of the 1970 ACM SIGFIDET (Now SIGMOD)
  Workshop on Data Description, Access and Control*, SIGFIDET ’70, páginas
  107-141, New York, NY, USA, 1970. ACM.
* Cormen, Thomas H., Charles Eric Leiserson, Ronald Linn Rivest y Clifford
  Stein: *Introduction to Algorithms, Third Edition*. MIT press Cambridge, 2009.
* Dijkstra, Edsger Wybe: *A note on two problems in connexion with
  graphs*. Numerische Mathematik, 1(1):269-271, 1959.
* Douglas, Alexander Shafto: *Techniques for the recording of, and reference to
  data in a computer*. The Computer Journal, 2(1):1-9, 1959.
* Gamma, Erich, Richard Helm, Ralph Johnson y John Vlissides: *Design patterns:
  Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software*. Addison-Wesley, Reading,
  Massachusetts, 1995, ISBN 978-0201633610.
* Goldman, Sally y Kenneth Goldman: *A practical guide to data structures and
  algorithms using Java*. CRC Press, 2007.
* Guibas, Leonidas John y Robert Sedgewick: *A dichromatic framework for
  balanced trees*. En *Foundations of Computer Science, 1978*, 19th Annual
  Symposium on, páginas 8-21. IEEE, 1978.
* Hoare, Charles Antony Richard: *Quicksort*. The Computer Journal, 5(1):10-16,
  1962.
* Jenkins Junior, Robert John: *Hash functions*. Dr Dobbs Journal, 22(9):107-+,
  1997.
* Knuth, Donald Ervin: *The Art of Computer Programming: Sorting and Searching*,
  volumen 3. Pearson Education, 1998.
* Liskov, Barbara Huberman y Jeannette Marie Wing: *A behavioral notion of
  subtyping*. ACM Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems (TOPLAS),
  16(6):1811-1841, 1994.
* Liskov, Barbara Huberman y Stephen Zilles: *Programming with abstract data
  types*. En ACM *Sigplan Notices*, volumen 9(4), páginas 50-59. ACM, 1974.
* Litwin, Witold: *Linear hashing: A new tool for file and table addressing*. En
  *VLDB*, volumen 80, páginas 1-3, 1980.
* Mehta, Dinesh: *Handbook of data structures and applications*. Chapman &
  Hall/CRC, Boca Raton, Fla, 2005, ISBN 1584884355.
* Williams, John William Joseph: *Algorithm 232 - Heapsort*, 1964.
* Wirth, Niklaus: *Algorithms + Data Structures = Programs*. Prentice Hall PTR,
  Upper Saddle River, NJ, USA, 1978, ISBN 0130224189.
